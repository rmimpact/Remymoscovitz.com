Sam Mary Moscovitz

Are you living the life you want?

At different stages of life, stress, anxiety and a general sense of discontent can begin to feel overwhelming. Therapy can support you to better manage stress, navigate life’s challenges, and gain a deeper understanding of yourself. It is a supportive space to reflect, grow, and create meaningful, positive change for the future.

Sam Mary Moscovitz is dedicated to supporting the mental wellbeing of her clients. She understands that each person is unique, with different needs at different times of their lives. Sam provides a safe, warm and relaxed environment where clients can feel comfortable and supported. Seeking therapy is not a sign of weakness, but a positive step towards strength, self-awareness and wellbeing.

The practice is conveniently located in the Inner West Sydney suburb of Concord, with easy access to public transport and ample parking available.
